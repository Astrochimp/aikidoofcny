About AIKIDO of CNY

At our Aikido school we provide our students with the best physical and mental discipline in Central New York.

We have incredible instructors, a wide variety of classes, and friendly, talented students.

Call us, email us, come in and chat with us! We would love to talk to you about our great school.

Classes

Samurai Action Class

Fun filled themes build strength and coordination, focus and discipline. Introductory Aikido techniques.

Aikido Youth Leadership Program

Martial Arts and conditioning for ages 9-14 emphasizing individual goals, confidence and leadership skills.

Aikido

Mixed rank adult classes for ages 15+ in the traditional martial art of Aikido

Sun Do

Traditional Korean art of breathing and postures for improved health
